Privacy

BarelyCamping · updated June 2026

Short version: this is just a landing page for my YouTube channel. I don't collect your personal info, I don't sell anything to anyone, and I don't run ads or trackers on you.

What this site does

It shows links to my socials, pulls in my latest YouTube videos, and has a little camping game. That's it. There's no account, no login, no contact form, no email signup.

Stuff stored on your own device

Your browser saves one small thing locally so the site behaves: a visitor number, so your camper number stays the same when you come back. That stays on your device. I never see it. Clear your browser data and it's gone.

Visitor count

To give each person a "camper #" the site pings a simple public counter. It just bumps a number up by one. No names, no emails, nothing that points back to you.

Other companies

The site is hosted on Vercel and the videos come from YouTube, so those services see normal web traffic the way every website does (basically your IP and browser, for the request to work). I don't get any of that, and you can read their own policies if you want the details.

Kids

This isn't aimed at collecting info from anyone, kids included, because I'm not collecting info, period.

Questions

Email me at hello@barelycamping.com and I'll answer.
